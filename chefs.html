Chefs

Gordon Ramsay
 Gordan Ramsay is a British chef born in Johnstone, Scotland but was raised in England. he made his own Resturant and gain 7 Michelin stars (Stars awarded to high standard resturaunts). Today he is one of the most known and most influential chefs in the UK.
Guy Fieri
 Guy Fieri was born in Columbus, Ohio in 1968. He co-owns three Restaurants in California and licenses his name to restaurants in New York City and Vegas. He is also host a telivision show which brings in more veiwers than any others on the Food Network (TV Channel).
Rachael Ray
 Rachael Ray was born in Glens Falls, New York. She host Cook shows on Food Network like 30 Minute Meals, which she shows great meals that take under 30 Minutes, Rachael vs. Guy Celebrity Cook-Off, Were Rachael Ray and Guy Fieri have a cook off against each other, and Rachael Ray's Kids Cook-Off, Were kids cook food and Rachael judges them. She also wrote many cook books.
Emeril Lagasse
 Emeril Lagasse was born in Fall River, Massachusetts. He is a celebrity chef, telivision personality, and cookbook auther. He was awarded National Best Recipe award winner for his "Turkey and Hot Sausage Chili" in 2003. He also has the regional James Bread Award winner. He makes a total of $l50 million annually off of his media, Products, and restaurants.
Bobby Flay
 Bobby Flay was born on December 10th, 1964 in New York City. He is owner and executive chef of many restaurants in Los Vegas, New York, and Alantic City. He has Restaurants in 11 diffrent states. He is also the host of several Food Network television programs.
